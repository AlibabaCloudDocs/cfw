# Release notes

This topic describes the release notes of Cloud Firewall.

## 2020

|Release date|Feature|Description|Involved edition|Documentation|
|------------|-------|-----------|----------------|-------------|
|2020-12|Policy ID for the Internet firewall|Policy IDs are provided for the Internet firewall, which helps you identify specific policies by their policy IDs.|All paid editions|[Search for a specific policy based on the policy ID](/intl.en-US/Access control/Access control for outbound and inbound traffic on the Internet firewall.mdsection_nsl_yli_zb2)|
|2020-12|Export of access control policies|Both inbound and outbound access control policies of the Internet firewall can be exported to your computer.|All paid editions|[Export policies](/intl.en-US/Access control/Access control for outbound and inbound traffic on the Internet firewall.md)|
|2020-11|Secure forward proxy|Outbound traffic from NAT Gateway to the Internet can be protected and controlled.|Enterprise Edition and Ultimate Edition|[t2004164.md\#]()|
|2020-11|Auto-renewal|Auto-renewal is supported by Cloud Firewall. This feature helps prevent service suspension if you do not renew your service on time.|All paid editions|[Enable the auto-renewal feature](/intl.en-US/Pricing and Service Activation/Renewal and upgrade.md)|
|2020-10|Notification settings|Notification settings are supported by Cloud Firewall. If Cloud Firewall detects abnormal traffic, compromised hosts, or suspicious outbound connections in your assets, it notifies you by text message or email.|All paid editions|[Configure notification and contact settings](/intl.en-US/.md)|
|2020-09|Safeguard mode for major activities|The Strict Protection Mode switch is added to the Toolbox page.|Enterprise Edition|[t1952831.md\#]()|
|2020-07|Configuration check of security groups|The feature used to check the security group configuration is added to the Toolbox page.|All paid editions|[Check security group rules](/intl.en-US/Toolbox/Check security group rules.md)|
|2020-06|Strict mode of access control policies|The Strict Mode switch is moved from the Internet Firewall tab of the Access Control page to the Toolbox page.|All paid editions|[Strict mode of the Internet firewall](/intl.en-US/Toolbox/Strict mode of the Internet firewall.md)|
|2020-06|Rollback of access control policies|The policy rollback feature is added to the Toolbox page.|Enterprise Edition and Ultimate Edition|[Back up and roll back an access control policy](/intl.en-US/Toolbox/Back up and roll back an access control policy.md)|
|2020-04|Breach awareness|Intrusion Detection is renamed Breach Awareness. The Breach Awareness page displays the intrusions detected by the intrusion prevention system \(IPS\).|All paid editions|[Breach awareness](/intl.en-US/Traffic Analysis/Breach awareness.md)|
|2020-03|Enterprise policy group|Enterprise policy groups are supported by internal firewalls.|Enterprise Edition and Ultimate Edition|[Access control on an internal firewall between ECS instances](/intl.en-US/Access control/Access control on an internal firewall between ECS instances.md)|
|2020-03|Strict mode of access control policies|The strict mode is supported by the Internet firewall.|All paid editions|[Strict mode of the Internet firewall](/intl.en-US/Toolbox/Strict mode of the Internet firewall.md)|
|2020-02|Mining activity over external connections|Mining activities are displayed on the External Connections page.|All paid editions|[Outbound connections](/intl.en-US/Traffic Analysis/Outbound connections.md)|
|2020-02|Most commonly encountered vulnerabilities|The most commonly encountered vulnerabilities are displayed.|All paid editions|None|
|2020-02|Alert|Cloud Firewall is connected with Cloud Monitor to display alerts.|All paid editions|None|
|2020-02|Internal firewall|Operations logs for internal firewalls are provided.|Enterprise Edition and Ultimate Edition|None|
